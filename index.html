Mujhe pata hai shayad aap mujhse naraz hain… aur agar sach kahun to main khud se bhi thoda naraz hoon. Kyunki jo insaan mere liye itna important hai, usi ko maine hurt kar diya.

Main perfect nahi hoon. Kabhi kabhi main situations ko samajhne me late ho jata hoon, kabhi sahi words choose nahi kar pata… par ek baat hamesha sach rahegi — aapko takleef dena kabhi bhi mera intention nahi tha.

Jab aap baat nahi karti, sab kuch thoda khaali sa lagta hai. Main arguments jeetna nahi chahta… main bas aapko khona nahi chahta.

Main yeh sab sirf maafi ke liye nahi likh raha… balki isliye, kyunki aapko khona mere liye option hi nahi hai.
